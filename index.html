Technical Sessions

Deep dive into cutting-edge Wi-Fi technologies

The Problems with Spatial Reuse in Wi-Fi 6 & 7

By Susinder Gulasekaran - Swinnovate

Spatial reuse is an optional new feature introduced in Wi-Fi 6 intended to increase the network efficiency in dense deployments. This talk gives insights into the reasons behind its zero adoption and highlights fundamental problems overlooked in WiFi 6 and WiFi 7.

802.11 PHY Evolution: Hits and Misses

By Srikanth Subramanian - Nanocell Networks

The WiFi success story has been built on evolving 802.11 standards. In this talk we will discuss what has clicked with respect to practice and some which have not worked well in practice.

Exploring Wi-Fi 7 Security Enhancements

By Rasika Nayanajith - Independent Expert

Explore how Wi-Fi 7 improves wireless security through mandatory WPA3 adoption, enhanced protections for Multi-Link Operation (MLO), and new features like Beacon Protection.

Real-Time Wi-Fi Testing & Behavioural Insights

By Shashank Tadakamadla - Cambium Networks

Real-Time Wi-Fi Testing simulates active user scenarios, mobility, and real-world interference — providing immediate insights into network behavior and stability.

Understanding EHT Preamble in WiFi 7

By Gjermund Raaen - Independent Expert

Explore how HE and EHT technologies use content channels to distribute RU allocation and user information in their preambles across different bandwidths and modes.

Nuts and Bolts of Wi-Fi and OpenWrt

By Shivam Thakur - Plume Design, Inc

Introduction to linux subsystem, Openwrt Architecture, WiFi 7 kernel frameworks, and live demo on Banana Pi R4 with Rust application for WiFi management.

Evaluating Wi-Fi Performance Under BLE Interference

By Manoj K, Yaswanth P, Venu Madhav M

Understanding and evaluating the impact of Bluetooth Low Energy interference on Wi-Fi performance with methodologies and tools to measure degradation.

Packet to Panel: Real-Time Network Traffic Analysis

By Nikhita K, Renusree R

A real-time dashboard that visualizes network traffic, latency spikes, and device-specific statistics, enabling live debugging of WiFi performance issues.

MLO Behaviour: Impact on Legacy Devices and Roaming

By Shyamala P, Kiranmai N, Rajendra P

Explore how Multi-Link Operation impacts legacy Wi-Fi devices and its implications for seamless roaming experiences in mixed network environments.

Looking Closer at the Client: A Deep Dive into STA side Testing

By Sainath P, Deepika B, Badhiri T

Understanding client behavior is critical. This talk explores techniques to simulate and analyze various client-side conditions and reactions.

Built for Engineers. Driven by AI. Introducing Candela Assist

By Vardhan S, Siva K, Sahithi U

A smart assistant platform offering real-time, voice-enabled and text-based assistance to guide users through complex Wi-Fi scenarios and LANforge test results.

Lab Promises vs Live Challenges: The Evolution of Wi-Fi Lab Testing

By Poojitha Y, Anmol S, Rudra S, Veena P

Exploring discrepancies between Wi-Fi performance in controlled labs and real-world deployments, and evolving methodologies to bridge this gap.

Turning Homes into Labs: Automating Wi-Fi Test Houses

By Akhil P, Saurabh G, Litin O, Md Aarif

Innovative approaches to transform residential environments into automated Wi-Fi test houses with cost-effective, scalable, and reproducible testbeds.
